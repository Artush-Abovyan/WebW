How we do it?

With WebWork - a time tracking and project management platform that has everything you need for efficient work and time management.

Why we do it?

A long time ago, when we were only a small team, we felt the need to use a time tracking tool across our team. We tried all the available tools, but to no avail.

Then We Had an Idea

We decided to create our own time tracker - one that would have everything the other tools were missing.

2016

WebWork Time Tracker was released - a fledgling time tracker with screenshots.

2017

Introduction of new feature - App & Website Usage Monitoring and Activity Monitoring.

2018

Then we added Project Management.

2019

Productivity Monitoring was the next new feature.

2020

We added Task management.

2021

Then Attendance Monitoring.

2022

We introduced Team Chat.

2022

Then HR tools: Leave and Holiday Management.

2022

More features are loading

Even though WebWork has grown and transformed into a more comprehensive tool, it still stays true to its main purpose - improved time management and productivity.

Our Values

These are what we believe in, what we practice, and what we preach.

People

We place people before employees and employees before everything else. Our employees are our biggest asset, and we make sure they feel appreciated for what they do.

On the other hand, our customers are of equal importance to us. We make sure to listen to them and provide them with the best service. In fact, we’ve built some of WebWork’s features based on our users’ requests and continue doing so.

Common Purpose

We started WebWork with a purpose - the purpose to make time management easier and better for everyone. While WebWork is growing, so is our purpose, but the core remains the same - to help others become productive and efficient at what they do.

Teamwork

We believe in the power of teamwork. In our experience, the best ideas are born in individuals but they are fulfilled in teams. We want to go far and go together.
